About Us

Neuralytic is a young start-up specialized in the development of Artificial Intelligence solutions with very high added value in the fields of Image Processing and Data Sciences.

Our level of expertise has enabled us to develop cutting-edge applications intended for medical.

We are working to develop an universal solution allowing us to perform any type of medical diagnosis, from heterogeneous sources:

  • digital images (x-rays, scanner, microscopes, skin pictures ...)
  • written records of medical analyzes
  • medical questionnaires
  • visual reading tests

This Artificial Intelligence Platform is dedicated to assist doctors, hospitals, crisis units, health ministries during:

  • usual consultations
  • telemedicine
  • heavy overflow to pandemics.
